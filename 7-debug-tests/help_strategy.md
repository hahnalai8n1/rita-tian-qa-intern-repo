# Problem-Solving Strategy

## Reflection

**When do you prefer using AI vs. searching Google?**
* **AI (ChatGPT/Claude):** I use AI when I need a complex concept explained clearly, or when I need to debug a specific error trace. AI acts as an excellent asynchronous mentor, providing detailed, structured explanations that I can process at my own pace.
* **Google:** I use Google for reading official documentation (like Pywinauto or Selenium docs) or finding highly specific bug reports on StackOverflow and GitHub Issues that require exact, historical context.


**How do you decide when to ask a colleague instead?**
I follow the 30-minute rule. If I have researched the error on Google and asked AI, and I am still completely blocked after 30 minutes, I will reach out. I will escalate by sending a clear, detailed written message outlining exactly what the issue is and what I have already tried.

**What challenges do developers face when troubleshooting alone?**
Tunnel vision is the biggest challenge. Staring at the same failing script can make you miss obvious syntax errors. Taking a step back—perhaps taking a movement break as suggested by Focus Bear—or writing out the problem clearly often reveals the solution.
