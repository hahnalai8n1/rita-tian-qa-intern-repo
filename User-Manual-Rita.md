# User Manual for Rita

### My Role
I am a QA Intern working on testing and quality assurance for our repositories.

### The days and times I work are:
Monday - Friday (Standard business hours, with flexibility).

### How I Work Best
* **I do my best work when...** I have clear, detailed instructions and the flexibility to manage my own schedule. I enjoy working in quiet spaces or environments with a light "buzz," like a coffee shop.
* **I struggle when...** Instructions are vague, or when I am pulled into frequent video calls and long, unstructured discussions.

### Communication Style
* **I prefer messages to be...** Written, clear, and direct. I value asynchronous communication because it allows me to process information thoroughly.
* **The best way to reach me is...** Through written messages (Discord, Slack, or Email).
* **If you need something urgent, do this...** Send me a direct message with a clear "URGENT" tag and the specific action item needed.

### Collaboration & Teamwork
* **I work best in a team when...** Expectations are documented in writing and everyone has the autonomy to execute their tasks.
* **My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is...** Relying solely on verbal feedback, as I may overlook details if they aren't written down.
* **If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means...** I am actively listening and processing the ideas being discussed. I need a bit of time to think before providing my best input.

### Focus & Productivity
* **I stay focused by...** Working in a quiet workspace or a café-like environment where I can concentrate without being interrupted by calls.
* **Things that distract me...** Sudden video calls or "quick" meetings that turn into long discussions.
* **The best way to support my productivity is...** To provide detailed project briefs and give me the space to dive deep into my work.

### Feedback & Growth
* **I like feedback to be...** Clear, direct, and provided in writing. This helps me refer back to it and ensure I’m improving.
* **When I make a mistake, the best way to let me know is...** A direct written message explaining what went wrong and how to fix it.
* **I appreciate it when teammates...** Give me time to process new ideas rather than expecting an immediate verbal reaction.

### Fun Facts & Quirks
* **One thing people often misunderstand about me is...** My silence in meetings isn't a lack of interest; it's just my way of processing information.
* **A fun fact about me...** I actually find the ambient noise of a busy coffee shop more productive than a completely silent, empty room!
* **My work superpower is...** Attention to detail and the ability to follow complex, detailed instructions to the letter.
